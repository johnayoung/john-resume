jyoung.dev — design explorations

Six full-site directions. Each includes a homepage, blog index, and the loop-engineering post, built with real site content. Round two (D–F) homepages are resume-first: full employment record, metrics, education, and one primary CTA.

Direction A

Manuscript

A typeset research journal. Warm paper, Newsreader serif, margin-note citations, an Abstract in place of TL;DR. Credibility through typography — almost zero motion.
